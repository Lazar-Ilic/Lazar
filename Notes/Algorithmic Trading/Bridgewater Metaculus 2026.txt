Final Comment

Thanks for another tournament. The sad news is that I was out of the money and kinda near the bubble. The happy news is that I learned a lot. I should try harder during hidden periods. This year, I had far too low variance on some of my predictions during those periods. Also, I should recall that "the market" is weighted by recent updates and in particular trolls can impact them a solid bit. So, there is the ongoing lesson in how to consider one's private information and edge with the market. My analysis is that for a big money tournament like this one it pays to go with higher|wider tails than the market usually in the logarithmic Brier scoring mechanism. But to get Top 10 one imagines going with the market too much will not work and one must find some real advantages over the other competitors.
